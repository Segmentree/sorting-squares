Level Editor

▶ Play
Tool

Click a cell to place. Click a slot again to rotate its open side (the dark wall marks the blocked back). With Tag, click a green box then a yellow slot to require that box to finish on that exact slot (a dotted line shows the link; click the box again to clear). A Red box roams free — it slips through other boxes and slot walls and never needs a slot (only holes and solids stop it). A Solid is a visible block nothing can pass. A Hole is invisible in‑game and blocks movement (shown hatched here).
